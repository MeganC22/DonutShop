About Us

After first opening its in the Spring of 2019, The Donut Shop is at its very best, brightest and most delicious.

Born and raised in Michigan, owner, Buz Gertz, a former heavy-weight boxing champion 4 years running, actually started the Donut Shop with his brother after retiring from the sport due to one too many blows to the head. And it is from such humble beginnings that Buz learned the lessons that would serve him in all of his business endeavors. As he told Goldpate News, "Donuts don't give you black eyes."

But don't take our word for it. Come in, grab a donut and see for yourself.
